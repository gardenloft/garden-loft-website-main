# gardenloft.ca

The **Garden Loft website** (https://www.gardenloft.ca) is the official online presence for Garden Loft — a company building dignified, modern micro homes for aging-in-place, supported living, and smart home independence.

This site showcases the Garden Loft vision, services, and technology, and serves as a hub for potential clients, families, and partners to learn more or get in touch.

---

## Purpose

- Present Garden Loft’s mission and values
- Highlight design, smart home features, and care-ready infrastructure
- Provide media, FAQs, and contact options for interested families, care providers, or municipalities
